MANISHA MALVIYA

The Director
Manisha Malviya represents the very finest in hair, skin and makeups, from the contemporary to the classic for over 26 years. She did Beauty Therapy & Hair Dressing Diploma back in the year 1992 from Womens World International headed by Shahnaz Hussain affiliated to International Therapy Examination Centre, London, UK.
With experience and influence gained from her time in Jodhpur and various other cities, Manisha Malviya provides clients with the highest standard of service in an unpretentious environment. “We want our salon to be a place where clients are treated with genuine warmth, a place where they can relax and feel good about themselves.”
